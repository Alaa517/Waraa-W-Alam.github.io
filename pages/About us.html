We at A Glance

Waraa W Alam is a Volunteering Youth initiative organizes events, activities and workshops for orphans to help and improve their technical and non technical skills and explore their hobbies.

Our vision

To compensate the absence of some life opportunities helping them to achieve thier goals.

Our mission

To give children the hope about the future. Seeking for live a better life by improving the quality of education and skills. We aim to increase the awareness of the society about the children's needs through various campaigns . Also, we Dream to RAISE A GENERATION WHICH CAN PROMOTE OUR NATION . All in all, we are an organization which helps Orphans by all means so as to improve their technical and non technical skills by offering them our experience to show them the prospects of life and how to deal with life difficulties in positive constructive ways . If you want to help in spreading the cause and achieving our goal contact us at hr.war2aw2lam@gmail.com or through our Facebook page.
